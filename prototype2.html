Description

Prototype 2 is an improved version of the first design. Based on the issues identified in Prototype 1, several modifications were made to improve functionality and usability.

The design includes better space for the ESP32 board, improved alignment for the lid, and additional openings to allow easier access to ports and pins.

Improvements from Prototype 1

  • Better fit for the ESP32 board
  • Improved lid attachment
  • Added openings for USB and pin access

Remaining Issues

  • Ventilation could still be improved
  • Lid may still loosen over time
  • Edges could be smoother

Reflection

Prototype 2 shows clear improvement compared to Prototype 1. The case fits better and is more usable, but further refinements are needed before reaching the final design.
