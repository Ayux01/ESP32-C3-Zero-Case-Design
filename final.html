Overview

The final design is the completed version of the ESP32 C3 case. It improves on both Prototype 1 and Prototype 2 by fixing earlier issues and creating a stronger, more usable case.

Key Features

  • Perfect fit for the ESP32 board
  • Improved ventilation for cooling
  • Easy access to USB port and pins
  • Stronger and more durable structure
  • Secure lid attachment

Future Improvements

Future versions could include waterproofing, better materials, and support for additional electronic components.
