About

This website documents our team’s journey in designing, testing, and improving a custom case for the ESP32‑C3 Zero. Across multiple prototypes, we explored how to improve fit, strength, USB access, pin access, ventilation, and overall usability for classroom projects. You can follow our full design process from Prototype 1, to Prototype 2, and finally to our Final Design.

Project Goal

The goal of this project was to create a case that fits the ESP32‑C3 Zero, protects it, allows easy USB and pin access, and includes ventilation for cooling.
